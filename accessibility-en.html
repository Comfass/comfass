Accessibility Statement

Accessibility Statement – Comfass

At Comfass, we place great importance on making our website and services accessible to the entire public, especially to people with disabilities.

The site was developed and designed in accordance with the Israeli Standard for Web Content Accessibility (IS 5568) and the international WCAG 2.1 Level AA, with the goal of providing a convenient, simple, and equal user experience for all users.

Accessibility Features on the Site

The site uses the external accessibility system “Nagish Li,” which enables, among other things:

  • Changing text size
  • Adjusting display colors, including high contrast
  • Highlighting texts and links
  • Control over display and highlights
  • Additional adjustments to assist users with visual impairments, reading difficulties, or other disabilities

Browser and Assistive Technology Support

The site is adapted for use in modern browsers and mobile devices, and supports screen readers and keyboard-only navigation.

Adjustments Implemented on the Site

  • Clear and hierarchical page structure
  • Alternative text for images and graphic content
  • Ability to change visual parameters in real time according to the user’s needs

Disclaimer

We invest significant efforts to make all pages on the site accessible; however, some content may not yet be fully accessible.

If you encounter any difficulty, we will be happy to receive your inquiry and address it as soon as possible.
